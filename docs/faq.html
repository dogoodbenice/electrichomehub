Getting Started

What is Electric Home Hub? +
Electric Home Hub is a centralized platform for managing smart home devices across all brands and manufacturers. We provide device tracking, documentation management, proactive issue monitoring, and developer APIs to simplify the fragmented experience of managing devices from multiple companies.
How do I add my first device? +
Navigate to the Device Tracking page and click "Add Device." You'll need your device's model number, serial number, and purchase date. Our system will automatically detect warranty information and set up monitoring for recalls and updates.
Is Electric Home Hub compatible with my existing smart home setup? +
Yes! Electric Home Hub works with devices from all major manufacturers including Samsung, Google, Amazon, Philips, TP-Link, and hundreds of others. We focus on management and monitoring rather than device control, so we complement your existing apps and systems.

Device Management

How does warranty tracking work? +
When you add a device, we automatically look up warranty information based on the model and purchase date. We'll send you alerts when warranties are about to expire and help you understand what's covered. You can also upload warranty documents for extended warranties.
What types of alerts will I receive? +
We monitor for device recalls, firmware updates, security patches, warranty expiration notices, and maintenance reminders. All alerts are prioritized by severity and include clear action steps. You can customize which alerts you receive for each device.
Can I organize devices by room or location? +
Absolutely! You can assign each device to a specific location (Kitchen, Living Room, Bedroom, etc.) and filter your device list by location. This makes it easier to manage devices and understand which areas of your home might be affected by alerts.

Developer API

How do I get started with the API? +
Visit our Developer API page to generate your first API key. We offer a free tier with generous limits, comprehensive documentation, and SDKs for popular programming languages. Our API allows you to integrate device management into your own applications.
What can I build with the API? +
Our API enables you to create custom dashboards, integrate with property management systems, build white-label solutions for your customers, or create specialized applications for specific industries like hospitality or real estate.
Is there a rate limit on API calls? +
The free tier includes 1,000 API calls per month. Paid plans start at $29/month for 10,000 calls. Enterprise customers can get custom rate limits. All plans include real-time monitoring of your API usage.

Data & Privacy

How is my data protected? +
We use enterprise-grade encryption for data at rest and in transit. All API access requires authentication, and we provide detailed audit logs. We never sell your data and only use it to provide the services you've requested.
Can I export my data? +
Yes! Use our Data Export feature to download all your device information, documents, and alert history in various formats including CSV, JSON, PDF, and Excel. You can also set up automated scheduled exports.
What happens if I want to delete my account? +
You can delete your account at any time from your settings page. We'll immediately stop all monitoring and permanently delete your data within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email when the deletion is complete.
